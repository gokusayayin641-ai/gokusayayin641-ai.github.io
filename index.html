The studio

Creating shouldn't be a privilege.

At Cloudy Sky we're dedicated to strengthening the Blender ecosystem through advanced extensions that improve its weak spots and expand its capabilities.

Our focus is on building innovative addons, designed to deliver high-end tools accessible to everyone.

We believe in an environment where you don't have to depend on what doesn't exist yet — you can build it. From that vision, this project was born, aimed at transforming the way creation happens inside Blender.

"To drive a future where creating isn't a privilege, but a possibility within everyone's reach."
Philosophy

Four principles. Zero compromises.

What guides every line of code and every product decision at Cloudy Sky.

/ 01

Professional quality

We build as if our own work depended on it — because it often does. No shortcuts, no "good enough".

/ 02

Real privacy

Your files are yours. 100% local processing whenever possible. Zero telemetry, zero hidden cloud uploads.

/ 03

No subscriptions

One-time payment. Forever. The tools you buy today will keep working tomorrow — no strings, no fees.

/ 04

Open ecosystem

We work inside Blender, not against it. Our addons feel native: same UX, same shortcuts, same philosophy.
